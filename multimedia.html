The Use Of Multimedia On The Web

Introduction

Multimedia is defined as a group of different elements forms such as text, audio, still images, animation and videos. According to Education Center Online, the word multimedia was first used in 1965 to describe an activity that includes music, lights, cinema and performance art. Technology has the biggest impact in the history of the development of multimedia. Furthermore, technology has expanded the definition and many people have an argument about how exactly the term should be used. The majority agreed that the term multimedia should be used to describe a product that contains several types of media. For instance, many websites have text, sounds, graphics, animation and videos.
