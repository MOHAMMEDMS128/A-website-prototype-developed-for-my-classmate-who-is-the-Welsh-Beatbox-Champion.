Basic Info

Ali Abed is a talented person in the field of beatboxing. He has been beatboxing for two years now. Furthermore, he is a member of A-Cloud beatbox tag team. Last year, was the most successful year for Ali and the A-Cloud tag team. Ali won The Welsh Beatboxing Championship and The Welsh tag team Beatboxing Championship. In November 2016, A-Cloud tag team gained the 2nd place for the UK Beatbox Championships. Currently, Ali is studying software engineering at Cardiff Metropolitan University.

Beatboxing (also beatbox, beat box or b-box) is a form of vocal percussion primarily involving the art of producing drum beats, rhythm, and musical sounds using one’s mouth, lips, tongue, and voice. It may also involve singing, vocal imitation of turntablism, and the simulation of horns, strings, and other musical instruments.
